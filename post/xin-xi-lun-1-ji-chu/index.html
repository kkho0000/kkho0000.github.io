【信息论】1. 基础

· 2023-01-07 · # 信息学 # 数学

基本定义

事件的自信息量

设xix_ixi​是一个事件，其发生的概率为P(xi)P(x_i)P(xi​)，含有的信息量是I(xi)I(x_i)I(xi​)

I(xi)=−log⁡P(xi)I(x_i) = - \log P(x_i) I(xi​)=−logP(xi​)

事件的互信息量

I(xi;yj)=I(xi)−I(xi∣yj)=log⁡p(xi∣yj)p(xi)\begin{aligned} I(x_i;y_j) &=I(x_i) - I(x_i \vert y_j) \\ &=\log\frac{p(x_i\vert y_j)}{p(x_i)} \end{aligned} I(xi​;yj​)​=I(xi​)−I(xi​∣yj​)=logp(xi​)p(xi​∣yj​)​​

信息熵

用于描述一个随机变量的不确定性，当随机变量的概率分布如下时

(XP)=(x1x2⋯xnP(x1)P(x2)⋯P(xn))\left( \begin{matrix} X \\ P \end{matrix} \right) = \left( \begin{matrix} x_1 & x_2 &\cdots &x_n \\ P(x_1) &P(x_2) &\cdots &P(x_n) \end{matrix} \right) (XP​)=(x1​P(x1​)​x2​P(x2​)​⋯⋯​xn​P(xn​)​)

信息熵如下定义

H(X)=E[I(X)]=∑i=1nP(xi)I(xi)=−∑i=1nP(xi)log⁡P(xi)\begin{aligned} H(X) &= E[I(X)] \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^n P(x_i)I(x_i)\\ &= -\sum_{i=1}^n P(x_i)\log P(x_i) \end{aligned} H(X)​=E[I(X)]=i=1∑n​P(xi​)I(xi​)=−i=1∑n​P(xi​)logP(xi​)​

联合熵

((X,Y)P)=((x1,y1)(x2,y2)⋯(xi,yj)⋯(xn,yn)P(x1y1)P(x2y2)⋯P(xiyj)⋯P(xnyn))\left( \begin{matrix} (X,Y) \\ P \end{matrix} \right) = \left( \begin{matrix} (x_1,y_1) & (x_2,y_2) &\cdots &(x_i,y_j) &\cdots &(x_n,y_n) \\ P(x_1y_1) &P(x_2y_2) &\cdots &P(x_iy_j) &\cdots &P(x_ny_n) \end{matrix} \right) ((X,Y)P​)=((x1​,y1​)P(x1​y1​)​(x2​,y2​)P(x2​y2​)​⋯⋯​(xi​,yj​)P(xi​yj​)​⋯⋯​(xn​,yn​)P(xn​yn​)​)

有如下联合熵

H(X)=∑i=1n∑j=1mP(xiyj)I(xiyj)=−∑i=1n∑j=1mP(xiyj)log⁡P(xiyj)\begin{aligned} H(X) &= \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m P(x_iy_j)I(x_iy_j)\\ &= -\sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m P(x_iy_j)\log P(x_iy_j) \end{aligned} H(X)​=i=1∑n​j=1∑m​P(xi​yj​)I(xi​yj​)=−i=1∑n​j=1∑m​P(xi​yj​)logP(xi​yj​)​

条件熵

H(Y ∣ X)=∑i=1nP(xi)H(Y∣X=xi)=∑i=1nP(xi)(−∑j=1mP(yj∣xi)log⁡P(yj∣xi))=−∑i=1n∑j=1mP(xiyj)log⁡P(yj∣xi)\begin{aligned} H(Y\,\vert \,X) &= \sum_{i=1}^n P(x_i)H(Y\vert X = x_i) \\ &= \sum_{i=1}^n P(x_i) \left( - \sum_{j=1}^m P(y_j \vert x_i)\log P(y_j\vert x_i) \right) \\ &= -\sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m P(x_iy_j)\log P(y_j\vert x_i) \end{aligned} H(Y∣X)​=i=1∑n​P(xi​)H(Y∣X=xi​)=i=1∑n​P(xi​)(−j=1∑m​P(yj​∣xi​)logP(yj​∣xi​))=−i=1∑n​j=1∑m​P(xi​yj​)logP(yj​∣xi​)​

平均互信息

I(X;Y)=∑i=1nP(xi)I(xi;Y)=∑j=1nP(xi)(∑j=1mP(yj∣xi)I(xi;yj))=∑i=1n∑j=1mP(xiyj)I(xi;yj)\begin{aligned} I(X;Y) &= \sum_{i=1}^n P(x_i) I(x_i;Y)\\ &= \sum_{j=1}^n P(x_i)\left( \sum_{j=1}^m P(y_j \vert x_i)I(x_i;y_j) \right)\\ &= \sum_{i=1}^n \sum_{j=1}^m P(x_iy_j)I(x_i;y_j)\\ \end{aligned} I(X;Y)​=i=1∑n​P(xi​)I(xi​;Y)=j=1∑n​P(xi​)(j=1∑m​P(yj​∣xi​)I(xi​;yj​))=i=1∑n​j=1∑m​P(xi​yj​)I(xi​;yj​)​

各种熵之间的关系

  • H(X,Y)=H(X)+H(Y ∣ X)=H(Y)+H(X ∣ Y)\begin{aligned} H(X,Y) &= H(X) + H(Y\,\vert\, X) \\ &= H(Y) + H(X\,|\,Y) \end{aligned} H(X,Y)​=H(X)+H(Y∣X)=H(Y)+H(X∣Y)​

  • H(X,Y)⩽H(X)+H(Y)H(X,Y) \leqslant H(X)+H(Y) H(X,Y)⩽H(X)+H(Y)

  • H(X ∣ Y)⩽H(X)H(X\, \vert \, Y) \leqslant H(X) H(X∣Y)⩽H(X)

  • I(X;Y)=H(X)−H(X ∣ Y)=H(Y)−H(Y ∣ X)\begin{aligned} I(X;Y) &= H(X)-H(X\,\vert\, Y) \\ &= H(Y)-H(Y\,\vert\, X) \\ \end{aligned} I(X;Y)​=H(X)−H(X∣Y)=H(Y)−H(Y∣X)​

  • I(X;Y)=H(X)+H(Y)−H(X,Y)I(X;Y) = H(X) + H(Y) - H(X,Y) I(X;Y)=H(X)+H(Y)−H(X,Y)

  • I(X;Y)⩽H(X)I(X;Y)⩽H(Y)I(X;Y) \leqslant H(X) \\ I(X;Y) \leqslant H(Y) I(X;Y)⩽H(X)I(X;Y)⩽H(Y)
